Banana Cream Pie

Description

In this recipe, you will make a creamy banana cream pie that suprise both yourself, and your family! When you dig into this home-mad delicacy, it will melt in your mouth like nothing else has.

TOP TIP:Keep your pie in the fridge until it has fully set!

Ingredients:

  • 150g of white sugar
  • 40g of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspon of salt
  • 475 ml of milk
  • 3 egg yolks, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1.25 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • One (9 inch) baked pastry shell, cooled
  • 4 sliced bananas

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 175Â°C
  2. Combine sugar, flour, and salt in a saucepan. Gradually stir in milk.
    Cook, stirring constantly, over medium heat until the mixture boils and thickens. Continue to stir and cook for 2 more minutes,
    then remove from the stove.
  3. Place egg yolks in a medium bowl. Whisk in a small amount of hot mixture, then
    immediately add egg yolk mixture to remaining hot mixture.
  4. Return the saucepan to the stove and cook, stirring constantly, for 2 more mintues.
    Remove the saucepan from the stove; stir in butter and vanilla.
  5. Fill pastry shell with sliced bananas. Cover with pudding.
  6. Bake until filling sets, 12 to 15 minutes.
  7. Chill pie for 1 hour before serving (or more if need be).
