Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

In this recipe, you will be making the best kind of chocolate chip cookies. They will be gooey. They will be crispy. And, most importantly, they will be DELICIOUS! We will be using a variety of ingredients to concuct this amazing dish that you can share with family and friends (and yourself!).

TOP TIP:Make sure you have chocolate chips - you can't make a chocolate chip cookie without one!

Ingredients:

  • Granulated sugar (100g)
  • Brown sugar (165g), packed
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • Unsalted butter (115g), melted
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • All-purpose flour (155g)
  • 0.5 teaspoon of bicarbonate soda
  • Milk chocolate chunks (110g)

Steps:

  1. In a larger bowl, whisk together the sugars, salt, and butter until
    a paste forms (make sure there are no lumps).
  2. Whisk in the egg and vanilla, beating until light ribbons fall of the
    whisk and remain for a short white before falling back into the mixture.
  3. Sift in the flour and baking soda, then fold the mixture with a spatula
    (Don't overmix, this would cause the gluten in the flour to toughen resulting
    in cakier cookies.
  4. Fold in the chocolate chunks, then chill the dough for at least 30
    minutes. For a more toffee-like flavour and deeper colour, chill the dough overnight.
    The longer it rests, the more complex the flavour will be.
  5. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C; line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  6. Scoop the dough with an ice-cream scoop onto the parchment paper-lined
    baking sheet, having at least 10cm of space between cookies and 5cm of space from
    the edges of the pan so the cookies spread evenly
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are starting to brown
  8. Cool completely before serving, enjoy!
