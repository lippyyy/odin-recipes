Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

In this recipe, you will be making the best kind of chocolate chip cookies. They will be gooey. They will be crispy. And, most importantly, they will be DELICIOUS! We will be using a variety of ingredients to concuct this amazing dish that you can share with family and friends (and yourself!).

TOP TIP:Make sure you have chocolate chips - you can't make a chocolate chip cookie without one!

Ingredients:

  • Granulated sugar (100g)
  • Brown sugar (165g), packed
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • Unsalted butter (115g), melted
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • All-purpose flour (155g)
  • 0.5 teaspoon of bicarbonate soda
  • Milk chocolate chunks (110g)
