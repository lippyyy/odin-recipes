Spaghetti and Meatballs

Description

In this recipe, you will be making a classic, spagheti and meatballs! This dish can be made with low cost ingredients with the same amazing result as a 5 star meal!

TOP TIP:Make sure you thoroughly season your meatballs to get all the flavours to shine.

Ingredients:

For the sauce:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 brown onion, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp dried mixed herbs
  • 400g tin tomatoes
  • 500ml vegetable/beef stock (from a cube)
  • 400g dried spaghetti
  • Salt and pepper

For the meatballs:

  • 400g beef mince
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp dried mixed herbs
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
